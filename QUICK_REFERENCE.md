## 🎙️ Use Case 9: Fluency & Accent Training

### What It Teaches:
🎯 **Stress Patterns** - Which syllables to emphasize
📍 **Sentence Chunking** - Break into natural thought groups
🔄 **Speaking Rhythm** - Sound more natural
⏱️ **Pacing** - Slow down for clarity

### Example:
```
You say: "I am really excited about going to university"

Coaching:
1. Break into chunks: "I am really excited / about going / to university"
2. Stress: "ex-CITE-ed (second syllable), u-NI-ver-si-ty (second syllable)"
3. Rhythm: "Don't rush - pause between chunks for natural flow"
```

### Where to See It:
- In **Pronunciation** mode
- When saying longer sentences
- In the **"Show Tips"** section

## 📖 Use Case 10: Storytelling Practice

### How It Works:
1. Select **Storytelling** from "Learning Focus"
2. Get a random prompt (or refresh for another)
3. Tell your story naturally
4. Get detailed analysis and praise
5. See specific improvements

### Available Prompts (10 total):
- "What did you do yesterday?"
- "Describe your dream vacation"
- "Tell me a childhood memory"
- "What's an interesting experience?"
- "Describe your favorite person"
- "A time you felt proud"
- "Your favorite holiday"
- "Tell me about your family"
- "A challenge you overcame"
- "Your biggest dream"

### What You Get Back:
✅ **What you did well** - Specific praise
✅ **Improvement suggestions** - Exactly where to add more
✅ **Overall assessment** - Encouragement
✅ **Metrics** - Word count, variety, emotion

### Example Analysis:
```
Your Story: "I went to school. I studied. It was fun."

Analysis:
✅ Clear and organized
💡 Add more description and emotion
💡 Use stronger, more descriptive words

Assessment: "💪 Great start! With more detail, your stories will be amazing!"
```
